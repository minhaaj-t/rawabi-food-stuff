About Al Rawabi (الروابي)

Wholesale Food Suppliers
Serving Qatar with Quality & Value

Al Rawabi Foodstuff - Wholesale Division is one of Qatar's most established and reliable wholesale suppliers, serving the nation's leading hypermarkets, supermarkets, groceries, hotels, restaurants, cafés, catering companies, and institutional buyers. With a strong distribution network and decades of market expertise, we supply a comprehensive portfolio of rice, pulses, spices, sugar, oils, canned items, dry food essentials, and specialty wholesale products sourced from trusted international and regional partners.
  • Consistent quality and competitive wholesale pricing
  • Timely nationwide delivery and strong distribution network
  • Trusted supplier to hypermarkets, hotels and institutional buyers
